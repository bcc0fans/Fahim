About Us

Our Founding Journey

Savar Cantonment Public School and College (SCPSC) started its journey on 2nd February 1977. It was recognized as Junior High School on 1st January 1978 and as Secondary School on 1st January 1980. In 1987 the institution was named Savar Cantonment Public School. Subsequently, college section of the institution started functioning since 1st July 1993.

Leadership & Governance

The General Officer Commanding, 9 Infantry Division and Area Commander, Savar Area is the Chief Patron of the institution and the Commander, 9 Artillery Brigade is the Chairman of its governing Body.

Our Distinguished Location

Savar Cantonment Public School and College situated in the suburb of Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is blessed with an excellent panoramic view easily capable of captivating the young minds. It is surrounded by the Savar golf course and very close to the national mausoleum - a pride, a glory and a lasting reminder of the great liberation war of Bangladesh and the Dhaka-Aricha highway almost equally bisects the two wings of the institution.

Our Educational Philosophy

Its discipline, co-ordinated educational system and timely planned activities are widely known to each and every corner of the whole Savar area. It is a representative institution in Dhaka district that is giving its tireless effort to impart a balanced education in a very congenial atmosphere to make worthy and morally upright citizens capable of facing the new century challenges.

Recognition & Achievement

This institution has occupied a prominent, proud and envious position for its academic results, co-curricular and extracurricular activities, skilled management and unique organizational characteristics.

Our Commitment

It is earnestly believed that this institution will further be enriched and march forward with the advice, instruction and guidelines from and concerted effort of all concerned.

Academic Programs

Primary Section (Class I-V)

Foundation building with emphasis on basic literacy, numeracy, creative thinking, and personality development through interactive and child-friendly teaching methodologies.

Junior Secondary (Class VI-VIII)

Comprehensive curriculum covering all core subjects with emphasis on developing analytical thinking and study habits for future academic success.

Secondary Section (Class IX-X)

SSC preparation with specialized focus on Science, Commerce, and Arts streams following National Curriculum and Board requirements.

Higher Secondary (Class XI-XII)

HSC programs in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams preparing students for university admission and professional careers.

Computer Science & ICT

Modern computer education with programming, web development, and digital literacy skills using latest technology and software.

Language Programs

English language proficiency, Bangla literature, and Arabic studies to develop communication skills and cultural understanding.

Science & Mathematics

Strong foundation in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics with practical laboratory work and problem-solving approach.

Arts & Humanities

History, Geography, Civics, and Fine Arts programs fostering creativity, cultural awareness, and critical thinking skills.

Commerce & Business

Accounting, Business Studies, and Economics preparing students for business careers and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Cafeteria

Transportation
